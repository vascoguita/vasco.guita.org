Customer-facing role leading onboarding and adoption of SonarQube Cloud and SonarQube Server for SonarSource's enterprise accounts. Run regular calls with customer technical and business stakeholders to architect implementation plans and drive renewals and license upgrades.

### Key Responsibilities

- Lead recurring customer calls to gather requirements, present solutions, and guide stakeholders through implementations.
- Architect implementations across on-premises, AWS, Azure, Docker, and Kubernetes, integrating CI/CD and SCM (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Azure DevOps) and SSO/identity systems (Entra ID, Active Directory, Okta) with JIT and SCIM provisioning.
- Configure Quality Gates, Portfolios, permissions, and Advanced Security (SAST, SCA, security and regulatory reports) to match customer requirements.
- Troubleshoot setups and advise stakeholders on configuration and integration decisions.
- Author Terraform templates and learning content for the SonarSource learning platform.
